About Me

Currently attending a coding boot camp. I'm a mechanical engineer by trade. However I want to chime into the medical field. I fancy robotic prosthetic limbs and wish to be able to place them onto patients. It's no serect that access to these technologies are not very economically friendly. With the raise of 3d printing, I hope to be able to provide more accessibility to families with disadvantaged backgrounds. During my spare time I volunteer for "Level the Cruve" which helps wheel chair users with hand dexterity issues.
